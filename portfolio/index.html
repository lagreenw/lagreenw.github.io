ABOUT ME

I am a multimedia artist, musician, and student based in Los Angeles and Washington State. In 2013 I started the band, RCV, which I currently manage, write, record, and perform with. In 2018 I began studying at the University of Southern California as a Media Arts + Practice (BA) major. My program focuses on interdisciplinary, socially-minded, and forward thinking approaches to media creation and analysis - which is great for me because I have too many interests to count!
