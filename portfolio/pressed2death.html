PRESSED 2 DEATH, (2018)

I created this music video for the local band, Illuminati Hotties. I started by creating a brighter, cartoonier, and more playful visual language for the band that still kept continuity with their previous visual material. My intentions with the animation were to reflect each of the instruments with visual elements on screen and create an overall feeling of being on a rollercoaster.
